ANALYSIS OF RESULTS
From the tests, it has been seen that the second output (Vo2) works properly and gives a suitable output most of the time.

On the other hand, even though at the beginning the first output (Vo1) appeared to work well, this changed along the course of the testing process, as it can be seen in the figure.

Once all the tests were finalised, the first thoughts were that there appeared to be a problem with the regulation of the first output using the potentiometers. However, after a careful revision of the PPU prototype, it was found that the ferrite transformer arrived at a saturation point due to a non-desired capacitive coupling that also affected one of the diodes.
